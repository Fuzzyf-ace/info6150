This website is about some Perissodactyla animals. Perissodactyla is a group of herbivorous mammals characterized by the possession of either one or three hoofed toes on each hindfoot.

The order includes about 17 species divided into three families: Equidae (horses, asses, and zebras), Rhinocerotidae (rhinoceroses), and Tapiridae (tapirs).
